The DigiNerd Network is a fansite dedicated to Digimon with a focus on the new Digimon TCG. The site is a tool for browsing the released cards, discussion, news, and fanfiction in the form of DigiFiction. The primary goal of the fiction is to tell interesting and engaging stories revolving around the card game and divergent from the narrative of the Digimon animated series. We hope you enjoy the site along the path to Digivolving Your Game!
